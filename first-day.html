How to Make a Sandwich

  1. Get a plate
  2. Get two slices of your favorite bread
  3. Place the slices on the plate
  4. Get a knife, a jar of PB and a jar of J
  5. Using the knife, spread the PB onto one side of one slice of bread
  6. Clean the knife.
  7. Using the knife, spread the J onto one side of the other slice of bread
  8. Place the two slices of bread together, taking care to place sides with the PB and J together
  9. Eat the sandwich
